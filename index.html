Football

The rules for the game:
Guess a word that is related to football. This word may be an NFL team or a position of a player on the field. You have 8 incorrect quesses before you loose and must restart the game. Everytime you answer a word correct, you will earn a win and the word will reset along with the number of incorrect guesses you have remaining. If you are stumped on a word, you can hit the "give-up" button and have the word revealed to you. Also, at any time you can hit the "start-over" button to reset all stats and start the game over with! Have fun!
